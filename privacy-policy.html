PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated January 21, 2026



This Privacy Notice for Albert Bradshaw (doing business as support@buzz-kill.app) ('we', 'us', or 'our'), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ('process') your personal information when you use our services ('Services'), including when you:

  • Download and use our mobile application (Buzz-Kill)

  • Use Buzz-Kill. BuzzKill is a mobile spending tracker that helps users monitor and control their expenses in real-time. Users create time-limited tracking sessions (e.g., 2 hours, 4 hours, full day) and can set optional spending budgets. The app securely connects to users' bank accounts through TrueLayer's open banking API to monitor pending transactions during active sessions. Key features include: Real-time spending tracking via secure bank account integration Live Activity updates on iOS lock screen showing current spending and time remaining Budget alerts that notify users when they reach specified spending thresholds (50%, 75%, 90%, or exceed their budget) Session history showing past spending patterns Group sessions allowing multiple users to track shared spending together Shareable session summaries for accountability The app processes bank transaction data to calculate total spending during sessions and sends push notifications to keep users informed of their progress. All financial data is encrypted and stored securely using Firebase's cloud infrastructure.

  • Engage with us in other related ways, including any marketing or events
Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. We are responsible for making decisions about how your personal information is processed. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.
